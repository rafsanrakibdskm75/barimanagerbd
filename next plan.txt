Advanced Auto Monthly Due System for “Bari Manager BD”

Feature Name:
“Smart Auto Rent Generation System”

Main Goal:
The app should automatically generate monthly rent dues for every occupied flat without manually creating collection records every month.

How It Works:

1. Auto Monthly Due Generation

* At the beginning of every new month:

  * System automatically creates a new rent due
  * No manual entry required
* Only for flats where:

  * Tenant exists
  * Flat status = Occupied

2. Previous Due Carry Forward
   If previous month rent is unpaid:

* Previous due automatically carries into next month
* New month rent gets added with old due

Example:
April Due = ৳5000 (Unpaid)
May Rent = ৳5000+5000(yellow) 

Total Due in May:
৳10000

3. Smart Due Preview
   Each flat card should show:

* Current Month Rent
* Previous Due
* Electricity Bill
* Water Bill
* Service Charge
* Total Payable
* Payment Status

4. One-Click Collection
   No manual record creation needed.

User only clicks:
“পাওয়া গেছে”

Then system automatically:

* Marks payment as collected
* Saves date/time
* Saves collector info
* Changes status to Paid
* Updates dashboard analytics
* Creates history log automatically

5. Auto Monthly Record System
   Every month system auto-generates:

* Rent Collection Entry
* Meter Calculation Entry
* Due History
* Pending Status

6. Smart Status Logic
   If unpaid:

* Status = Pending

If partially paid:

* Status = Partial

If fully paid:

* Status = “পাওয়া গেছে”

7. Auto Month Change Detection
   When month changes:

* App automatically checks:

  * Existing occupied flats
  * Previous pending amounts
* Then creates new month records automatically

8. Auto Collection History
   Every payment should automatically save:

* Month
* Amount
* Collector
* Payment method
* Payment date
* Remaining due

9. Dashboard Auto Update
   Dashboard automatically updates:

* Monthly income
* Pending amount
* Paid flats
* Unpaid flats
* Total due

10. Offline Support
    Even without internet:

* Monthly due auto generation still works
* Data saves locally
* Auto sync to server later

11. Smart Toggle in Settings
    Settings Option:
    ☑ Enable Auto Monthly Rent Generation

If enabled:

* System auto creates dues monthly

If disabled:

* Admin creates manually

12. Additional Smart Features

* Auto rent generation on first app open of new month
* Duplicate month prevention
* Automatic month label:

  * January 2026
  * February 2026
* Auto late fee support (optional future feature)

UI Behavior:

* Large “পাওয়া গেছে” button
